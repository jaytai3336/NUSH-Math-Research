Q1 JOURNAL
"Some Functional Upper Bounds for Fejér's Sine Polynomial"
Studia Scientiarum Mathematicarum Hungarica
Published · 2021  ·  ISSN: 0081-6906
This paper establishes sharp functional upper bounds for Fejér's classical sine polynomial F_n(x) = Σ sin(kx)/k. Fejér's polynomial arises naturally in approximation theory, harmonic analysis, and the study of positive trigonometric polynomials. We derive new closed-form upper bound expressions and prove their sharpness by characterising the equality cases. The methods combine classical analysis techniques with careful estimation of partial sums of trigonometric series.

Q2 JOURNAL
"A Sharp Trigonometric Double Inequality"
Publicationes Mathematicae Debrecen
Published · 2021  ·  ISSN: 0033-3883
We establish a sharp double inequality of the form L(x) ≤ f(x) ≤ U(x) for a composite trigonometric expression. The bounds are shown to be tight — achievable at specific interior points of the domain — rather than merely asymptotically sharp. The proof employs a combination of Taylor expansion analysis, monotonicity arguments, and careful interval arithmetic to verify the bounds uniformly over the entire domain.
